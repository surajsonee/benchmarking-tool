Here's how it works.

Using census data, we can predict a neighbourhood's average energy consumption, and by uploading your utility bill, we can confirm your address and show your area's energy models. With your help, we can start making more accurate models about your home. Our AI will provide you with smart suggestions that will make you save on your bill and making you more environmentally green. It's a win-win!


Who is Pollen?

Pollen is a start-up that is spearheading the home energy modelling industry, founded by people passionate about sustainable energy. Our goal is to provide homes with information on energy distribution and how everyone can easily switch to cheaper and greener options. We are aiming to launch early in 2021, and we aim to significantly reduce Greenhouse Gas Emissions by converting households to more energy efficient alternatives.
